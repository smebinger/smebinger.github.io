See New Hampshire's 4,000 Footers!

There are 48 mountain peaks in New Hampshire that are at least 4,000 feet in height and meet the criterion of topographic prominence, as determined by the Appalachian Mountain Club. All 48 mountain peaks are within mountain ranges that comprise the White Mountains in northern New Hampshire. Zoom in and click on a mountain on the map for more information.
